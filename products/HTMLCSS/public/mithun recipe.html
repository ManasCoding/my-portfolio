Mithun's recipe for the best cinnamon rolls

ingredients

for the dough:

  • 4 cup warm milk
  • 2 teaspoons yest
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 2 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

for the fillings:

  • 3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup butter

instructions

  1. mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. melt the butter and add to the mixter.
  3. add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. knead the dough for 10 minuits.
