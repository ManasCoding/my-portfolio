Biography

Steven paul jobs (february 24, 1955 - october 5, 2011) was an american enterpreneur, industrial designer, business magnate, media proprietor, and invester. he was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of apple; the chairman and majority shereholder of pixar; a member of the walt disney company's board of directers following its acquisition of pixar; and the founder, chairman, and CEO of NeXT. he is widely recognized as a pioneer of the personal computer revolution of the 1970s, along with his early business partner and follow apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.
